Why Electrical Utilities Choose nSight

Lightning-Fast Network Navigation & Search

  • Instantly locate any substation, feeder, pole, transformer, or consumer using intelligent auto-complete search across millions of records

Powerful Spatial Analysis & Selection

  • Draw points, lines, rectangles, circles, or polygons and instantly filter any network layer within the geometry
